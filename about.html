The Director

Dr. Arvinder Pal Singh

Director & Principal Instructor

With a passion for teaching and a wealth of knowledge, Arvinder Pal Singh is the driving force behind Harvard Computer Classes. His journey in computer science spans over 20 years, blending advanced academic research with hands-on industry experience.

He holds a MCA, PGDCA, Msc. CS and has worked on complex software systems and network architecture. This unique combination allows him to not only teach the 'how' but also the 'why' behind critical concepts, giving students a significant competitive advantage.

Our course content is constantly updated to reflect the latest trends and demands of the job market, making you job-ready upon completion.
